Dr.Meredith Grey

Head of General surgery
Board Director
Director of the Residency Program

Meredith Grey is the Head of General Surgery and Director of the Residency program at Grey Sloan memorial Hospital. Her mother was Dr.Ellis Grey, the famous now deceased General Surgeon. She was married to Dr.Derek Shepherd until his death and they have 3 children together. She has also won a Catherin Fox Award.
